Clothing Swap

to Benefit Planned Parenthood & She Should Run

4834 7th Street NW, Washington DC
Saturday, May 30, 2020 at 1pm - 5:30pm

Have clothing you want to get rid of?! GREAT! We would love to take them! Check out the FAQ below, as well as information on where and when to drop off clothes!

What are you looking for?

We are seeking women's clothing of any size. These clothes should be gently worn (no giant holes or stains, etc), and laundered.

We are also looking to collect accessories (purses, shoes, jewelry, belts, scarves, etc).

But I can't make the swap. Do you still want my clothes?

OF COURSE! We'd love to take your stuff off your hands!

What will you do with the clothing after the swap?

All clothes at the end of the swap will be donated to a local organization. If you would like to volunteer to take a load of clothing, please indicate this on the sign up sheet.

How do I donate?

Great! We'd thought you'd never ask. Please place all clothing (folded nicely) in bags separated into the following categories:

  • Skirts
  • Pants/shorts
  • Dresses
  • Tops
  • Sweaters
  • Jackets
  • Business suits
  • Accessories

Please label the outside of the bag with both the category and the size. So for example, one bag would say, "Skirts, sizes L to XL." Another bag would be "Sweaters, sizes XL to 2X." Please try to lump clothing in similarish sizes. (So put small tops in a different bag than XXL tops). This will help to expedite the day-of set up.

Please make sure shoes have the size on there somewhere. (You can always add a post-it note in the shoe if it's not readable on there).

I also have a ton of hangers and racks. Interested?

OH YES!!! We very much need hangers, clothing racks, mirrors, and shelves to display clothes! You can drop off all of these items at the below locations as well. NOTE: getting clothes and hangers BEFORE the event itself is a huge help.
